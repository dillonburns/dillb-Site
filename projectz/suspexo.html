suspexo
This was a logo destruction and protest project. In 2014 Sodexo changed what qualifies as a full-time position, causing a majority of employees at RPI to cease to recieve health benefits, matching contributions towards their 401ks, no sick time, vacation time, or retirement funds. Sus as fuck if you ask me. I modified their logo and placed these fliers in the napkin dispensers around campus to raise awareness. The QR code lead to a news article detailing the situation.
